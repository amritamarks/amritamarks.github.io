about: amrita marks
my mind is a jungle
My portfolio of work is mainly from NCEA Level 3 Design. Creating for a non existant landscape design company, I expolored ideas of transforming spaces into a hideaway. I wanted my work to convey feelings of tranquility and playfulness through various photos and vector imagery.
portfolio

photography
These photos brought my visions of jungle landscapes and a feeling of escapism to life. The rich shades of green highlight the garden background, which is the main focus of the images.

business cards
I explored geometric shapes in my business cards, which related to the natural shapes found in nautre. Bringing these shapes through allowed me to use less practical and obvious imagery, but still related to the brand.

magazine covers
My magazine covers were one of the first things I designed for this brand. I experimented with a range of vector imagery and photography, colours and shapes. The brand was called ‘AM Landscapes’, after my initials, but I played with the idea of ‘am’ relating to mornings, and the feelings that come at this time.
magazine spreads
In my magazine spreads, I focused more on combining sucessful elements such as geometric shapes, particular colours relating to certain emotions, photos and geometric shapes. These elements all helped to communicate the style of the brand.

poster
This poster was not related to my landscape brand. ‘Headspace’ is an art exhibition that showcased student work at my school. The photography used in the background was by another student who was displaying work at the exhibition.
